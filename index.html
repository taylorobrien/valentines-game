Catching Love

  • High Concept: You are flying through the air to catch hearts. Watch out for the broken ones!
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should feel the game simple, but frustrating due to the slow movement of the character, and getting trapped.
  • Genre: Valentines Day Game.
  • Game play:In this prototype of Catching Roses, the player continuously jumps in order to grab the different colored hearts. They may continuously jump even when not on the ground. When the player collects hearts, they will receive 1 point, and when they collect broken hearts, they will loose 5 points. The player has 90 seconds to collect as many points as possible.
  • Prototype goal:This prototype is evaluating object collision, a timer, movement with spritesheets, sprites using spritesheets, stopping the game and gravity/jumping movement.
  • Features: The Art.
  • Setting/Premise: The player is in a pink sky catching hearts.
  • Story: The character is catching hearts to be loved for Valentines Day. If they catch the broken hearts, they will feel unloved on Valentines Day.
  • Target Audience: This game is intended for new-gamers, or gamers that enjoy simple game.
  • Play time: 90 Seconds
  • Strategies: Avoid Broken Hearts, Pay attention to the gravity and movement.
